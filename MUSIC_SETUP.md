# Adding Calm & Soft Background Music

## Quick Setup

To add your own calm, soft, feel-good music:

1. **Download a calm music file** (MP3 format recommended)
   - Look for: ambient, lo-fi, soft piano, acoustic, or peaceful instrumental music
   - Good sources: Pixabay Music, Free Music Archive, YouTube Audio Library

2. **Place the file in the `public` folder**
   - File name: `calm-background-music.mp3`
   - Full path: `public/calm-background-music.mp3`

3. **That's it!** The music player will automatically use your file.

## Music Recommendations

For a calm, soft, feel-good vibe, look for music with:
- ✨ Slow tempo (60-80 BPM)
- 🎹 Soft instruments (piano, strings, acoustic guitar)
- 🌊 Peaceful, ambient melodies
- 💝 Emotional but uplifting tone

## Features

- **Soft Volume**: Default set to 20% for a gentle background presence
- **Fade In/Out**: Music fades in and out smoothly for a peaceful experience
- **Auto-loop**: Music plays continuously
- **Controls**: Play/pause, volume slider, and mute button
- **Collapsible**: Hide/show controls as needed

Enjoy your calm, feel-good music! 🎵✨
